The mine descent
What you will do

Five hours, from gravel to gemstone

This is the real thing, not a staged attraction. You go underground into a working mine, see how the earth gives up a sapphire, then follow that stone through the market and the cutting wheel.

  • Welcome and a briefing from our head gemologist
  • Descend a working mine and collect gravel
  • Private gem market tour with live buying
  • Traditional cutting and heating demonstration
  • Gem museum and a complimentary lunch
